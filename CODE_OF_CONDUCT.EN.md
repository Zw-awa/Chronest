# Code of Conduct

[中文](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) | English

## Our standard

Chronest contributors are expected to keep discussion technical, respectful, and direct. Harassment, intimidation, discrimination, and bad-faith behavior are not acceptable.

## Expected behavior

- Assume good intent while reviewing concrete behavior.
- Disagree with ideas without attacking people.
- Keep feedback specific and actionable.
- Respect project scope, safety boundaries, and maintainer decisions.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Personal attacks
- Harassment or discrimination
- Deliberate disruption
- Publishing private information without consent
- Repeated bad-faith argument after decisions are documented

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, reject contributions, or ban contributors when necessary to protect the project.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues privately to the maintainers through GitHub or the security contact path listed in `SECURITY.EN.md`.
